# Roadmap

## Phase 1: Foundation
Focus on the highest-impact trust, accessibility, and conversion items first.

Recommended priorities:
- Clarify contact flow configuration
- Improve navigation accessibility
- Remove or verify any unsupported claims
- Tighten footer and trust links
- Establish documentation and governance

## Phase 2: Quality and Growth
Once the base experience is stable, improve:
- SEO depth
- Performance and motion budget
- Content specificity
- Proof and credibility structure
- Shared component consistency

## Phase 3: Expansion
After the core site is strong, consider:
- Dedicated service pages
- Industry pages
- Case studies
- Stronger SEO content systems
- CRO improvements
- AI automation as a future service only if it fits the brand

## How to Use This Roadmap
- Treat the roadmap as a sequencing guide, not a fixed commitment
- Do not start large redesigns without approval
- Prefer high-confidence improvements over speculative additions
- Keep every change aligned with the Vertex Digital brand
