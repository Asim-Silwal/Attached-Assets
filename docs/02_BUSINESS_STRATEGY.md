# Business Strategy

## Business Model
Vertex Digital is a service business focused on helping home service companies grow through website design, development, branding, SEO, and ongoing maintenance.

The business model should support long-term client relationships rather than one-off transactional work.

## Current Core Offerings
- Website Design
- Website Development
- UI/UX Design
- Branding
- SEO
- Website Maintenance

## Future Offerings
Potential future expansions include:
- Conversion Rate Optimization
- AI automation

These additions should only be introduced when the business has a clear delivery model for them and the brand can support them without confusing the main offer.

## Market Positioning
Vertex Digital should be positioned as:
- Premium value
- Strong process
- High trust
- High professionalism
- Strong understanding of home service businesses

The business should not compete primarily on price.
The goal is to be the better strategic choice, not the cheapest option.

## Revenue Strategy
The website and brand should support the following business outcomes:
- Discovery calls
- Qualified inbound leads
- Better conversion from existing traffic
- Long-term recurring work where appropriate

## Growth Strategy
- Focus on one vertical cluster at a time within home services
- Build deep credibility instead of broad generic positioning
- Improve proof, process, and trust before widening the market message
- Keep the offer simple and easy to understand

## Operational Strategy
The public website should reduce friction and create confidence.
The internal delivery model should emphasize:
- Clear requirements
- Reusable system parts
- Predictable launch process
- Reliable communication
- Measurable outcomes where possible

## Strategic Risks to Avoid
- Competing as a commodity agency
- Overloading the site with too many services or promises
- Overstating results without proof
- Building a brand that feels too trendy or too technical for the target buyer
