# SEO Strategy

## SEO Goal
SEO should help Vertex Digital be discoverable to U.S. home service business owners who are looking for a better website partner and a more credible digital presence.

## Current Baseline
The current site has a basic SEO foundation in its HTML shell, including title, description, robots directives, and social meta tags.
That is a good starting point, but it is not enough for a long-term growth strategy.

## SEO Priorities
- Improve metadata quality
- Strengthen page-level message relevance
- Add structured data where appropriate
- Create indexable content that matches search intent
- Improve internal navigation and content depth over time

## Content Strategy for SEO
Content should focus on:
- home service website design
- home service web development
- local SEO for contractors
- conversion-focused agency services
- trust-building and lead generation

## Technical SEO Principles
- Keep titles and descriptions unique where possible
- Maintain a clean canonical strategy
- Support fast page load and good Core Web Vitals
- Ensure images are described appropriately
- Use semantic headings and link structure

## Opportunity Areas
- Route-specific metadata if the site expands beyond a single page
- Location or industry pages if the business expands into stronger search marketing
- Case-study style proof content backed by real evidence
- Better social preview images and structured data

## What Not To Do
- Do not stuff keywords into the copy
- Do not create thin SEO pages
- Do not rely on vague generic agency language
- Do not publish claims that are not supported
