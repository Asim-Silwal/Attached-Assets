# Target Audience

## Primary Audience
Vertex Digital serves U.S.-based home service business owners.
The primary decision-maker is usually a business owner or operator who wants more calls, more quote requests, and a stronger online presence.

## Core Audience Profile
- Age range: roughly 35 to 60
- Role: owner, founder, operator, or principal decision-maker
- Mindset: practical, time-sensitive, results-oriented
- Priority: getting better leads, not learning web design

## Target Industries
Initial industry focus:
- Roofing
- Home Remodeling
- General Contractors
- HVAC
- Plumbing
- Landscaping
- Painting
- Electrical
- Flooring
- Kitchen & Bathroom Remodeling

## Audience Needs
This audience typically wants:
- More qualified leads
- More phone calls
- More quote requests
- A website that looks credible and modern
- A service provider who understands their business
- A clear process with low friction

## Audience Concerns
This audience may worry about:
- Paying for a website that does not generate work
- Working with a provider who does not understand home services
- Being sold generic marketing advice
- Complex process and poor communication
- A site that looks attractive but fails to convert

## What They Respond To
- Clear business outcomes
- Trust and credibility signals
- Industry familiarity
- Straightforward process explanation
- Proof of professionalism
- Simple next steps

## What They Do Not Respond To
- Flashy visuals with no substance
- Vague creative language
- Overly technical explanations
- Casual or childish tone
- Claims that feel exaggerated or unsupported

## User Journey Expectations
A visitor should quickly understand:
- Who Vertex Digital helps
- What problem Vertex Digital solves
- Why Vertex Digital is credible
- How the process works
- How to start a conversation
