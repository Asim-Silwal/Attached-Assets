# Content Guidelines

## Content Goal
Content should help a home service business owner quickly understand that Vertex Digital improves trust, conversion, and lead quality.

## Core Message
Vertex Digital helps home service businesses grow through better websites and digital experiences.

## Writing Principles
- Be direct
- Be specific
- Be confident without being boastful
- Keep language practical and business-focused
- Write for scanning, not for literary flair

## Approved Themes
- Trust
- Growth
- Lead generation
- Process
- Professionalism
- Industry understanding
- Better conversion
- Long-term partnership

## Avoid These Patterns
- Cheap pricing language
- Unsupported performance claims
- Hype without proof
- Jargon that does not help a business owner
- Overly casual or trendy phrasing

## Claims Policy
Do not invent client results, rankings, conversion rates, or revenue outcomes.
If a claim is used, it should be:
- verifiable
- clearly sourced
- approved for public use

If there is no proof, use a general statement instead of a specific metric.

## Page-Level Content Expectations
Each major page or section should answer at least one of these quickly:
- What problem is being solved?
- Why is Vertex Digital credible?
- What service is being offered?
- What should the visitor do next?

## Tone by Section
- Hero: clear and confident
- Services: practical and specific
- Portfolio/proof: factual and credible
- FAQ: helpful and reassuring
- CTA: simple and action-oriented

## Contact and Conversion Copy
Conversion copy should reduce friction.
Use language that makes the next step feel easy, safe, and worthwhile.
