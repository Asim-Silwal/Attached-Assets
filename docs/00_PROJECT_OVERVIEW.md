# Project Overview

## What Vertex Digital Is
Vertex Digital is a growth-focused digital partner for U.S. home service businesses.
The company builds websites and digital experiences that are intended to improve trust, generate discovery calls, and produce more qualified leads.

Vertex Digital is not positioned as a cheap website vendor.
The company should be understood as a premium, process-driven partner focused on business growth.

## Mission
Help home service businesses grow by building high-converting websites, improving their online presence, and creating digital experiences that generate more qualified leads.

## Current Audience
The initial market is the United States, with a focus on home service and trade businesses such as roofing, HVAC, plumbing, landscaping, electrical, remodeling, painting, and flooring.

## Current Product Shape
The current implementation is a marketing website built with a React/Vite frontend and a small Express API service.
The site is designed as a single-page conversion experience with sections for hero messaging, credibility, services, portfolio, trust-building, process, FAQs, and a contact CTA.

The codebase also includes shared packages for API schema and client generation, which suggests a broader platform direction even though the visible product is still primarily a marketing site.

## Current Strategic Priorities
- Increase trust and conversion quality
- Present a premium but calm brand
- Explain the process clearly
- Focus messaging on home service business growth
- Keep the experience easy to scan and easy to navigate
- Build a foundation that can scale into a real business rather than a one-off site

## What Should Guide Future Decisions
Any new feature or redesign should be evaluated against these questions:
- Does it help a home service business get more leads?
- Does it make Vertex Digital more trustworthy?
- Does it preserve the clean, premium, minimal brand?
- Does it improve clarity and conversion without unnecessary complexity?
