# PROJECT_CONTEXT

## Purpose
Vertex Digital is not a generic web design agency. It is a growth-focused digital partner for home service businesses that need more qualified leads, more phone calls, and more quote requests.

The product is not websites alone. The product is business growth through better digital experiences.

This document is the single source of truth for future development decisions.

## Company
- Name: Vertex Digital
- Market focus: United States first
- Long-term direction: grow into a real, trusted business with durable systems, not a one-off portfolio site

## Mission
Help home service businesses grow by building high-converting websites, improving their online presence, and creating digital experiences that generate more qualified leads.

We do not sell websites.
We sell business growth.

## Target Clients
Primary audience:
- Business owners roughly 35–60 years old
- Owners who care about more customers, more calls, and more quote requests
- Decision-makers in home service and trade businesses

Initial target industries:
- Roofing
- Home Remodeling
- General Contractors
- HVAC
- Plumbing
- Landscaping
- Painting
- Electrical
- Flooring
- Kitchen & Bathroom Remodeling

## Brand Personality
Vertex Digital should feel:
- Reliable
- Professional
- Results-driven
- Modern
- Clean
- Minimal
- Trustworthy
- Calm
- Premium without feeling luxury
- Friendly but not casual

Avoid:
- Flashy visuals
- Gimmicks
- Overly futuristic aesthetics
- Anything that feels cheap, loud, or trend-chasing

## Design Philosophy
The visual direction should be inspired by companies like:
- Stripe
- Vercel
- Linear
- Framer

Design principles:
- Clean
- Spacious
- Consistent
- Easy to scan
- Minimal
- Premium

Guidelines:
- Use whitespace intentionally
- Keep layouts simple and readable
- Use animation to support the experience, not distract from it
- Avoid unnecessary effects
- Preserve clarity over spectacle

## UX Philosophy
Every page should quickly answer:
- Who are we?
- Who do we help?
- Why should someone trust us?
- What problem do we solve?
- What should they do next?

Navigation should always feel effortless.
The experience should reduce friction and help visitors move confidently toward a discovery call.

## Business Positioning
Vertex Digital is not competing on being the cheapest.

The brand should be perceived as:
- Premium quality
- Excellent value
- Professional
- Process-driven
- Focused on long-term partnership

Avoid language that suggests the company is inexpensive because it is based in Nepal.

Emphasize:
- Quality
- Results
- Professionalism
- Process
- Long-term partnership

## Services
Current services:
- Website Design
- Website Development

Future expansion may include:
- CRO
- AI automation

## Website Goals
The website should:
- Increase trust
- Generate discovery calls
- Show professionalism
- Clearly explain the process
- Position Vertex Digital as an expert in home service businesses
- Build credibility for international clients

## Technical Goals
When making code changes:
- Keep the code modular
- Avoid unnecessary complexity
- Prefer reusable components
- Keep accessibility in mind
- Optimize for performance
- Avoid breaking existing functionality
- Preserve the current visual identity unless improvements are approved

## Future Development Rules
Before implementing any feature:
1. Understand why the feature exists.
2. Keep consistency with the existing design system.
3. Suggest improvements if something conflicts with the brand.
4. Never make large redesigns without approval.
5. Explain technical decisions clearly.

## Working Agreement
This document should guide future product, design, content, and engineering decisions.
If a proposed change conflicts with this context, pause and confirm the direction before implementing it.
